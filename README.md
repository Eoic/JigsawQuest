# JigsawQuest - multiplayer jigsaw puzzle solving game.

### Features 
* **Play Solo or Multiplayer**: Choose to tackle puzzles on your own or invite friends to join you in multiplayer mode. Collaborate or compete to see who can solve the puzzle the fastest.
* **Achievements**: Unlock a wide range of achievements as you progress through the games. Test your skills and strive to earn them all.
* **Statistics**: Track your progress and puzzle-solving skills with detailed statistics. Monitor your completion times, number of pieces placed, puzzles solved, and more to improve your performance.
* **Leaderboards**: Compete with players from around the world and climb to the top of the leaderboards. Compare your scores and puzzle-solving abilities with other players.
* **Custom Images**: Bring your own images to the game and turn them into puzzles. Personalize your gaming experience by solving puzzles using your favorite photos or artwork.
* **Custom Rules**: Create unique challenges by setting custom rules for your games. Adjust the number of puzzle pieces, rotation options, and other parameters to make each game truly unique.
